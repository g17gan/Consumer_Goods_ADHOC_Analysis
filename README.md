# 📊 Consumer Goods Ad-Hoc Analysis | SQL + Power BI

## Overview

This project focuses on solving real-world business problems for AtliQ Hardware, a global consumer goods company. The analysis was performed using SQL to extract business insights from a data warehouse, and Power BI was used to create visualizations for management reporting.

The objective was to answer ad-hoc business requests from executive management and transform raw data into actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Executive management required quick insights into product performance, customer behavior, sales trends, manufacturing costs, and channel effectiveness.

Using SQL queries, key business questions were answered and visualized in Power BI to support data-driven decision-making.

## Database Structure

The analysis was performed on the **atliq_hardware_db** data warehouse consisting of:

| Table Name                  | Description                          |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| dim_customer                | Customer details and segmentation    |
| dim_product                 | Product hierarchy and classification |
| fact_sales_monthly          | Monthly sales transactions           |
| fact_gross_price            | Product pricing information          |
| fact_manufacturing_cost     | Product manufacturing costs          |
| fact_pre_invoice_deductions | Customer discount information        |

## SQL Business Requests Solved

### 1. Markets for AtliQ Exclusive in APAC Region

Identified all APAC markets where AtliQ Exclusive operates.

### 2. Unique Product Growth Analysis

Compared unique products sold in 2021 versus 2020.

**Key Insight:**

* Unique products increased by 36%.
* 89 additional products were introduced in 2021.

---

### 3. Product Segment Analysis

Analyzed product count by segment.

**Key Insight:**

* Notebook segment leads with 129 products.
* Accessories follow with 116 products.
* Networking has the lowest product count.

---

### 4. Segment Growth Analysis

Compared product counts by segment between 2020 and 2021.

**Key Insight:**

* Accessories showed the highest growth (+34 products).
* Desktop segment experienced significant expansion.

---

### 5. Manufacturing Cost Analysis

Identified products with highest and lowest manufacturing costs.

**Key Insight:**

* Highest Cost Product: AQ HOME Allin1 Gen 2
* Lowest Cost Product: AQ Master wired x1 Ms

---

### 6. Customer Discount Analysis

Analyzed top customers receiving the highest average pre-invoice discounts in India during FY 2021.

**Key Insight:**

* Flipkart received the highest average discount.
* Major e-commerce customers received discounts close to 30%.

---

### 7. Monthly Gross Sales Analysis

Analyzed monthly gross sales for AtliQ Exclusive.

**Key Insights:**

* November 2021 recorded peak sales.
* Significant growth observed throughout FY 2021.
* Early 2020 showed reduced sales activity.

---

### 8. Quarterly Sales Performance

Evaluated sold quantity by quarter in FY 2020.

**Key Insight:**

* Q1 recorded the highest sales volume.
* Q3 experienced a major decline before recovery in Q4.

---

### 9. Channel Contribution Analysis

Measured revenue contribution by sales channel.

**Key Insights:**

* Retailers contributed 73% of total sales.
* Direct channel contributed 15%.
* Distributors contributed 11%.

---

### 10. Top Products by Division

Identified the top 3 products by sold quantity within each division.

**Key Insight:**

* N&S Division significantly outperformed other divisions.
* AQ Pen Drive 2 IN 1 was the highest-selling product overall.
